Pasta Delight

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta (any type)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil or butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (pureed)
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • Salt & pepper to taste

How to Make:

  1. Boil pasta as per packet instructions and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil or butter in a pan, sauté garlic and onions until soft.
  3. Add tomato puree, chili powder, salt, and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Mix in boiled pasta, stir well, and let it absorb flavors.
  5. Top with grated cheese and serve hot!

Cheesy Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza base (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce or ketchup
  • 1 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup capsicum (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp oregano or mixed herbs

How to Make:

  1. Preheat oven or tawa (pan).
  2. Spread tomato sauce evenly on the pizza base.
  3. Layer mozzarella cheese and chopped capsicum.
  4. Sprinkle oregano on top and bake for 15 minutes at 180°C (or cook covered on a tawa for 10 minutes).
  5. Slice and serve hot!

Refreshing Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cucumber (diced)
  • 1 cup tomato (diced)
  • 1/2 cup onion (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt & black pepper to taste

How to Make:

  1. Chop all ingredients and place them in a bowl.
  2. Drizzle with lemon juice and mix well.
  3. Add salt and black pepper for taste.
  4. Serve fresh as a side dish or appetizer!

Grilled Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp mustard or lemon juice
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric & chili powder
  • 1 tbsp yogurt (for marinade)
  • Salt to taste

How to Make:

  1. Mix all ingredients and marinate the chicken for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat a grill or pan, cook chicken for 7 minutes per side.
  3. Drizzle lemon juice before serving.
  4. Enjoy your juicy grilled chicken!
